Jessie Mordine Young

Jessie Mordine Young is a Brooklyn-based artist who researches, writes, curates, produces, and teaches textile art. She has recently begun planning a “Weaving Challenge” of daily artworks, 100 woven artworks on frame looms in 100 days. These weaving samples are linked to her experiences in nature, where color and texture become tangible references to memory, and intimacy is felt in the work. She specializes in incorporating materials such as metal, shells, dried flowers and leaves into the fibers, interspersing the structure to present a variety of textures and forms with a unique visual language.

“Without realizing it, I find objects that make the woven work feel complete.”
